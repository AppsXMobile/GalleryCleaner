Introduction

Welcome to Gallery Cleaner! This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data and protects your privacy. We are committed to transparency and want you to understand exactly what happens with your photos and personal information when you use our app.

Gallery Cleaner is a local photo management application designed to help you find and remove duplicate photos from your Android device. The app is developed by Herso App and is available on the Google Play Store.

Our Privacy Commitment

Core Privacy Principle:

Gallery Cleaner is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles. All photo analysis, duplicate detection, and processing happens entirely on your device. We do NOT upload, transmit, or share your photos with any external servers or third parties.

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be absolutely clear, Gallery Cleaner does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Photos or Images: Your photos never leave your device
  • Location Data: We don't access or store GPS coordinates or location information
  • Device Information: No device IDs, hardware specs, or unique identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of how you use the app
  • Crash Reports: No automatic error reporting or diagnostics
  • Advertising Data: No ad tracking or user profiling

Permissions Explained

Gallery Cleaner requires certain permissions to function properly. Below is a detailed explanation of each permission, why we need it, and how we use it:

READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+)

Required

Purpose: Access your photos and images

Why We Need It: To scan your device for photos, detect duplicates, and display them in the app interface.

How We Use It: The app reads photo files from your storage, analyzes them using AI algorithms to find similarities, and presents duplicate groups for your review.

Scope: Photos only - no videos, documents, or other files are accessed.

READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+)

Optional

Purpose: Future feature support for video thumbnails

Current Status: Requested but not actively used in version 1.0.0

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)

Required on older Android

Purpose: Legacy permission for accessing photos on Android 10-12

Note: Automatically replaced by READ_MEDIA_IMAGES on Android 13+

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 10 and below)

Legacy

Purpose: Delete photos on older Android versions

Note: Only used on Android 10 and below. On newer versions, deletion is handled through MediaStore API.

MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 11+)

Required

Purpose: Full access to photos across all folders

Why We Need It: To scan photos in all folders (Camera, Screenshots, WhatsApp, Downloads, etc.)

How We Use It: This allows the app to find duplicates across your entire photo library, not just specific folders.

Security: This is a sensitive permission, but necessary for the app's core functionality. We only access image files.

ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION

Optional

Purpose: Read EXIF metadata from photos

Why We Need It: To extract date/time information for better duplicate detection

Privacy Note: We read metadata locally but don't store or transmit location data.

INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Technical Requirement

Purpose: Required by .NET MAUI framework

Actual Usage: NOT actively used by the app. No network requests are made.

Note: These are standard permissions included by the development framework but the app works 100% offline.

How Gallery Cleaner Works

Understanding how the app works will help you see why your privacy is protected:

1. Photo Scanning

  • You select which folders to scan (Camera, Downloads, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • The app reads photo files from your device storage
  • All scanning happens locally on your device
  • No photos are uploaded or transmitted anywhere

2. Duplicate Detection

  • Photos are analyzed using Perceptual Hashing (pHash) - an AI-powered algorithm
  • The algorithm creates a unique "fingerprint" for each photo
  • Similar fingerprints indicate duplicate or similar photos
  • All processing happens on your device's CPU - no cloud processing

3. User Review

  • Duplicate groups are displayed for your review
  • You see similarity percentages (e.g., 98% similar)
  • You manually select which photos to keep or delete
  • The app only deletes photos when you explicitly tap "Delete"

4. Deletion Process

  • Deletion requires your explicit confirmation
  • A confirmation dialog asks "Are you sure?"
  • Photos are permanently deleted from your device storage
  • No backup copies are created (you should backup important photos)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only:

  • The app stores temporary data in your device's app-specific storage

  • All data is stored locally and deleted when you uninstall the app

Security Measures:

Gallery Cleaner Nasıl Çalışır

Uygulamanın nasıl çalıştığını anlamak, gizliliğinizin neden korunduğunu görmenize yardımcı olacaktır:

1. Fotoğraf Tarama

  • Hangi klasörlerin taranacağını seçersiniz (Kamera, İndirilenler, WhatsApp vb.)
  • Uygulama, cihaz depolama alanınızdaki fotoğraf dosyalarını okur
  • Tüm tarama işlemi yerel olarak cihazınızda gerçekleşir
  • Fotoğraflar hiçbir yere yüklenmez veya aktarılmaz

2. Yineleme Tespiti

  • Fotoğraflar Algısal Karma (pHash) kullanılarak analiz edilir - yapay zeka destekli bir algoritma
  • Algoritma her fotoğraf için benzersiz bir "parmak izi" oluşturur
  • Benzer parmak izleri, yinelenen veya benzer fotoğrafları gösterir
  • Tüm işleme cihazınızın CPU'sunda gerçekleşir - bulut işleme yok

3. Kullanıcı İncelemesi

  • Yineleme grupları incelemeniz için görüntülenir
  • Benzerlik yüzdelerini görürsünüz (örn. %98 benzer)
  • Hangi fotoğrafları saklayacağınızı veya sileceğinizi manuel olarak seçersiniz
  • Uygulama yalnızca siz açıkça "Sil" düğmesine dokunduğunuzda fotoğrafları siler

4. Silme İşlemi

  • Silme işlemi için açık onayınız gerekir
  • Bir onay iletişim kutusu "Emin misiniz?" diye sorar
  • Fotoğraflar cihaz depolama alanınızdan kalıcı olarak silinir
  • Yedek kopya oluşturulmaz (önemli fotoğraflarınızı yedeklemelisiniz)
